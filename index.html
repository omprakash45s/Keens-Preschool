Welcome to Keens Preschool

We believe young children learn best in a happy relaxed atmosphere. Our goal is to provide your children with tools necessary to begin their formal education. The innovative Preschool Curriculum will help us plan learning experiences that are just right for your child that they can learn on their own pace.

Our program enriches holistic Development, where the children experiences interactive literacy and thematic learnings through play and this strengthens social, intellectual and physical skills so the children eventually enhances their language, math, science, social studies, gym, dance and art lessons.
