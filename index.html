Welcome to Keens Preschool

We believe young children learn best in a happy relaxed atmosphere. Our goal is to provide your children with tools necessary to begin their formal education. The innovative Preschool Curriculum will help us plan learning experiences that are just right for your child that they can learn on their own pace.

Our program enriches holistic Development, where the children experiences interactive literacy and thematic learnings through play and this strengthens social, intellectual and physical skills so the children eventually enhances their language, math, science, social studies, gym, dance and art lessons.

Our Programs

Explore our age-appropriate learning programs designed to meet the developmental needs of children from toddlers to preschoolers. Each program is tailored to nurture individual growth.

Playgroup

Our Playgroup program is designed to introduce children aged 2 to 3 years to a interactive learning environment.

Nursery

In our Nursery program, children aged 3 to 4 years embark on an exciting journey of exploration and fun-filled learning.

LKG

At the LKG level, we provide a stimulating and child-centric approach to education for ages 4 to 5 years.

UKG

Our UKG program, designed for ages 5 to 6 years, emphasizes academic readiness and personal growth.

Daycare

Our Daycare program offers a safe and nurturing space for children aged 1 to 3 years.
